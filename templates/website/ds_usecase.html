Data Science Use Cases


Client Path to Loyalty

Understanding how your client moves through your business and the behaviours and purchases that are distinguished between client paths to identify the virtuous path to loyalty and potential opportunities along it.



Omni-Channel Interactions

Linking digital interactions, pre-sale actions and behaviours with online and offline sales to determine the main digital paths and to provide a holistic view of your customer journey and enhance your communication strategy across channels



End to end Journey clustering

Capitalize on the richness of the omni-channel journey in order to identify the path with the highest value, and to identify opportunities for personalization to reduce churn. This requires an in-depth analysis of the customer journey and clustering across comparable journeys to identify the next best action at each significant touchpoint



Personalization through Scoring

Develop a customer scoring algorithm to measure the propensity of your clients to visit the store, churn, acquire a certain product, cross-sell, become VIP… The learnings from the scoring are then fed through into your communication strategy in order to boost the specified KPI



Advanced performance measurement

Conducting a sophisticated measurement program to accurately analyze the performance of different CRM actions whilst creating valuable deep-dives in order to adapt your customer centric strategy

Network Clustering Solution

Define key typologies of your network, according to the characteristics and behaviors of their customers. These typologies will be the basis with which to build a more personalized in-store experience through the sales ceremony and boutique assortment. It will enable the setting up of objectives, performance monitoring at a boutique and sales associate level.



Brand image perception

Build a brand image index which incorporates multiple dimensions, whilst accurately measuring the impact of the specified dimensions. The approach is adapted to answer your business questions and data availability. Results will be treated to produce operational insights on brand image.
